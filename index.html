About O C Music

O C School of Music have been teaching and bringing music to peoples lives for the best part of a decade. We teach everything from a vast amount of instruments to singing and music production. With our skilled teachers you can learn to play Drums, Guitar, Piano, Violin and Harp and we also have talented vocal and music production tutors on staff.
