About O C Music

O C School of Music have been teaching and bringing music to peoples lives for the best part of a decade. We teach everything from a vast amount of instruments to singing and music production. With our skilled teachers you can learn to play Drums, Guitar, Piano and Violin and many more. We also have talented vocal and music production tutors on staff. Along side our fully qualified staff you will learn everything from the basics right up to being a session musician.We will give you the confidence to play your music in front of a live crowd as we host 2 annal concerts every year to showcase your talent and to ready you for live events. So feel free to go to our sign up page fill in the info and what you would like to learn and we will get back to you promptly!
